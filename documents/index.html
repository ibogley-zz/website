The second is Public Policy: I am passionate about using my experience to better the lives of others. What better way to help others succeed in the modern world than to communicate the external trends that are affecting their communities? Being able to understand what has been happening in the current globalized world is the best weapon against being powerless to change, and public policy itself can add crucial support where needed.
